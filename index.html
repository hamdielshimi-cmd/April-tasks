ValU Partnership

1. Triple Zero Campaign

A financial empowerment initiative designed to make high-end medical care accessible through a strategic partnership with ValU.

  • The Core Offer: 0% Down payment, 0% Interest, and 0% Administrative fees.
  • Installment Flexibility: Patients can choose to spread payments over 6, 12, or 24 months.
  • Scope of Service: Valid for all medical services provided by the group.
  • Geographic Focus: Targeted specifically at patients across Cairo and Giza.
  • Campaign Rule: Cannot be combined with any other discounts or price-reduction promotions.
⏱ Duration: Positioned as a "limited time" offer spanning 3 months.

Top Care Premium

2. Beauty & Dental Voucher

An initiative focused on the "Top Care" premium unit, utilizing a "Professor-Led Integrated Hub" concept to cross-promote luxury services.

  • Promotional Mechanism: Patients who book a premium eye procedure receive a free consultation voucher for either the Dental or Dermatology (Beauty) departments.
  • Strategic Objective: Aims to solve the "fragmented patient journey" by integrating premium ophthalmology, dermatology, and dentistry under one luxury roof.
  • Target Audience: Designed for Class A patients and medical tourists seeking a boutique, high-end experience.
  • Premium Benefits: VIP experience including a private entrance and a 10-minute maximum waiting time guarantee.
  • Positioning: Marketed as "Egypt's First Professor-Led Luxury Medical Hub," leveraging the academic authority of Professor Ihab Saad Othman.

2. عرض الـ Voucher (تجميل وأسنان)

مبادرة تركّز على وحدة "Top Care" الفاخرة، وتستخدم مفهوم "المركز المتكامل بقيادة أستاذ جامعي" للترويج المتقاطع للخدمات الراقية.

  • آلية العرض: المرضى الذين يحجزون عملية عيون فاخرة يحصلون على كوبون كشف مجاني في قسم الأسنان أو الجلدية (التجميل).
  • الهدف الاستراتيجي: حل مشكلة "رحلة المريض المجزأة" من خلال دمج طب العيون والجلدية والأسنان الراقية تحت سقف واحد.
  • الفئة المستهدفة: مرضى الفئة A والسياح العلاجيين الباحثين عن تجربة فاخرة ومتميزة.
  • المزايا الفاخرة: تجربة VIP تشمل مدخلًا خاصًا وضمان الانتظار لمدة لا تتجاوز 10 دقائق.
  • التموضع التسويقي: يُسوَّق باعتباره "أول مركز طبي فاخر بقيادة أستاذ جامعي في مصر"، مستفيدًا من مكانة الأستاذ الدكتور إيهاب سعد عثمان.

WE Offer

4. Telecom Egypt (WE) Employees Offer

A special medical insurance offer exclusively for Telecom Egypt (WE) employees at Donia Al Oyoun Hospital, covering ophthalmology consultations, radiology, and surgical procedures at preferential rates.

  • Coverage: Consultations, radiology, and all ophthalmic surgical procedures.
  • Eligibility: All active Telecom Egypt (WE) employees enrolled in the company medical insurance scheme.
  • Booking: Call 16456 to reserve an appointment.
  • Validity: Active until further notice.
